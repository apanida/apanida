Meet Sarah and Mark

Hello there! We’re Sarah and Mark Thompson, a married couple with an insatiable passion for exploring the world, one adventure at a time.

For us, travel isn’t just about ticking off destinations; it’s about immersing ourselves in diverse cultures, connecting with incredible people, and collecting memories that last a lifetime.

Our story began in Boston, where we both grew up and met during college. With shared dreams of traveling, we spent years exploring locally and gradually expanded our adventures internationally.
